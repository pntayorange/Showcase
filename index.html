Curing Analysis Paralysis.

A gamified, visual-first food discovery app. Swipe right to like, left to pass. Decide what to eat in under 5 minutes.

🍔 Stop arguing over dinner. Join the waitlist today.

Why Choose Tinner?

Current delivery apps overwhelm you with endless lists and text, causing decision fatigue. Tinner changes the game by focusing purely on visual appeal and quick decisions.

  • Swipe To Bite

    One dish at a time. Swipe right if it looks good, left to pass. Gamifying the selection process.

  • Personal Collections

    Save your matched restaurants into custom lists for date nights, weekend hangouts, or late cravings.

  • 1-Tap Navigation

    Seamlessly route to your chosen restaurant via Google Maps with just a single tap.

  • Save Time

    Our ultimate goal: Go from opening the app to deciding on a meal in under 5 minutes.

Core Experiences

Designed to remove all friction from deciding what to eat, leaving only the fun part: enjoying the food.

SINGLE-VIEW UI

Say goodbye to endless scrolling & information overload.

By displaying only one restaurant at a time, Tinner drastically reduces cognitive load. It removes distractions so you can focus purely on what matters: how good the food looks.

  • Tinder-like intuitive card swiping.

  • Large, immersive food photography.

SEAMLESS JOURNEY

We remove the friction from your dining experience.

Tinner isn't just about finding food; it's about getting you there. Once you've made a match, we provide everything you need to start your journey immediately.

  • Visual proximity map to see what's nearby.

  • Instant API integration with Google Maps.
